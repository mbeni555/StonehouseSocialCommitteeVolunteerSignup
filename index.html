About Us

We are the Stonehouse Owners Foundation Social Committee dedicated to bringing neighbors together and creating meaningful experiences that celebrate what makes our community special. We organize seasonal events, social gatherings, and community activities that help us connect with one another and build a stronger Stonehouse community. Whether you live in Fieldstone Estates, Glen, Townes, Ridge, Manor, Reflections, or one of our future sections, this is your space to share, celebrate, and support your neighbors.

What to Expect

Our committee welcomes neighbors from all SOF communities who want to help plan and execute neighborhood events throughout the year. We welcome members with varying levels of involvement—from planning meetings to hands-on event execution. We're here to help each other and keep things positive and respectful as we build connections together.

Meeting Details

  • When: First Monday of every month
  • Time: 6:30 PM
  • Attendance: Not mandatory—participate when you can!
  • Commitment: Volunteer for at least 1 event per year
