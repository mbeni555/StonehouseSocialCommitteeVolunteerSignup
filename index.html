About Us

We are a community-focused social committee dedicated to bringing neighbors together and creating meaningful experiences. We organize seasonal events, social gatherings, and community activities that foster connection and belonging.

What to Expect

Our committee plans and executes a variety of neighborhood events throughout the year. We welcome members with varying levels of involvement—from planning meetings to hands-on event execution.

Meeting Details

  • When: First Monday of every month
  • Time: 6:30 PM
  • Attendance: Not mandatory—participate when you can!
  • Commitment: Volunteer for at least 1 event per year
